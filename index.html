Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How often should I work out?

    A: It depends on your goals and fitness level, but 3-5 days a week is a good place to start for most people.

  • Q: What should I eat to build muscle?

    A: A diet rich in protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates will help you build muscle. Try to include lean meats, eggs, and whole grains.

  • Q: Do I need supplements to build muscle?

    A: Supplements can help but are not necessary. Focus on a balanced diet and consistent training. If you do take supplements, protein powder and creatine are popular choices.

  • Q: How long does it take to see results?

    A: You can start noticing changes in strength within a few weeks, but visible results usually take a few months, depending on your starting point and consistency.
